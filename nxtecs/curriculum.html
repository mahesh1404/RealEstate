Course Overview

AEM Training from NXTECS teaches you the essential skills needed to develop and debug Adobe Experience Management through best practices. The training starts with AEM basics and dives into central concepts like architecture, installation, features, using AEM with Maven and VLT, etc. By the time you’re done with this certification training, you’ll have the required skill set to tackle advanced development challenges in AEM.
