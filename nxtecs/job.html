For an AEM Architect

       Our company is growing rapidly and is looking for an AEM architect. Thank you in advance for taking a look at the list of responsibilities and qualifications. We look forward to reviewing your resume.

Responsibilities for AEM architect


• Establishes auditing procedures to ensure continued integrity
• Assist in post-implementation continuous improvement efforts in enhancing performance and providing increased functionality
• Serves as team leader in the integration of solutions, constructing applications, developing new business opportunities and building relationships with clients
• Research and coordinates conversions and upgrades to in-house and vendor systems
• Works with IT operations, incident and problem management teams in resolving issues and maintaining application availability and stability in production
• Develop workflows, build new templates and application customizations for the WCM application
• Define and document best practices to manage performance and scalability of the content management and content delivery solution
• Follow Secure DLC process to develop in accordance with security requirements and best practices coding procedures
• Contributes to project plans for large projects and supports their implementation
• Develop proposals for implementation and design of Web Content Management solutions, based on Adobe AEM

Qualifications for AEM architect


• Experience in HTML5, DHTML, CSS3 and Javascript (ExtJS, jQuery )
• Domain expertise in managing and supporting enterprise class systems
• Minimum 1 year Agile delivery experience
• 3+ years of experience developing ecommerce or web content management applications
• A minimum of a Master’s degree or equivalent in IT, Engineering or related field is required
• Professional experience in a global organization is also required
